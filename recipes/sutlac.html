Description

Rice pudding, which you can consume hot or cold, is a type of dessert you can choose both in summer and winter.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter of milk
  • 2 tea glasses of rice
  • 1 liter of water
  • 3 tablespoons rice flour
  • 1.5 -2 cups of granulated sugar
  • 1 glass of milk
  • cinnamon

Steps

  1. To make rice pudding, first wash the rice and put it on the fire with water.
  2. Boil until the rice stretches and absorbs the water. Be careful not to burn the rice by turning the stove on too high a heat.
  3. Then add cold milk. Stir it 1-2 times and wait for it to boil.
  4. Meanwhile, in a bowl, crush the rice flour with 1 glass of cold milk. Make sure that there are no lumps left.
  5. Take 1-2 scoops of boiling milk in the pot and add it to the bowl. (rice flour should be warm).
  6. Add the rice flour to the pot and cook for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Add granulated sugar, mix and boil for 1-2 more minutes.
  8. Divide the rice pudding into bowls.
  9. When the rice pudding has cooled, you can serve it by sprinkling cinnamon on it. Enjoy your meal.
