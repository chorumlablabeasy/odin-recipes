Menemen Recipe


Home Page


Description

Menemen is a traditional Turkish dish that is generally consumed for breakfast and loved by everyone.


Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 green peppers
  • 1 capia pepper
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 4 eggs
  • one teaspoon of salt
  • one teaspoon of black pepper

Steps

  1. Put the olive oil in a non-stick pan and fry the finely chopped onion until it turns pink.
  2. Then add green peppers and capia peppers and continue roasting.
  3. When the peppers start to wilt, add the tomatoes you cut into cubes.
  4. Keep the lid gaps open until the tomatoes become soft.
  5. When the onions turn pale, break the eggs, add the spices and mix.
  6. Stir until the eggs are cooked, then turn off the stove and serve the menemen hot.
